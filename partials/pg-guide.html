1. Overview

Flock is a pastoral call-tracking tool designed to help you stay consistently connected with the people in your care. It keeps track of who you have called, when each person is due for a follow-up, and what was discussed — so nothing falls through the cracks.

Flock works by connecting to your contact list (stored in a Google Sheet) and showing you a prioritised call schedule each day. Every interaction you log is saved automatically.

💡Flock works best when you log calls promptly after finishing them — this keeps the dashboard accurate and your next due dates up to date.
🏠
2. Home Screen

The Home screen is your daily starting point. At a glance you can see three key numbers:

● Callbacks — people waiting for a return call ● Overdue — past their due date ● Due Today — scheduled for today

Tap any stat box to jump straight to that section in the Who to Call dashboard. From Home you can also navigate to every other section of the app.

The ⚙ Settings icon (top right) opens app and people settings. The moon icon toggles dark mode.

📋
3. Who to Call

This is your prioritised call schedule. People are sorted into sections based on urgency:

● Callbacks ● Overdue ● Due Today ● This Week ● No Date Set

The coloured strip at the top shows a count for each category — tap a category to jump to it. Tap Log next to any name to open the Log a Call form with that person pre-filled.

People scheduled more than two weeks out are hidden — they will appear automatically when the time comes. Pull down to refresh the list.

💡Work through Callbacks first, then Overdue, then Due Today. This keeps your most urgent commitments covered.
📞
4. Logging a Call

Use this form to record the outcome of any call. Here is the flow:

1
Select a person — type their name in the search box and tap to select. Any recent interactions will appear below for context.
2
Pick a result — choose one of: Reached, No Answer, Left Message, or Rescheduled.
3
Set next action — choose None, Callback (they will call you back), or Follow-up (you will call them again). Choosing Callback or Follow-up reveals a date & time picker.
4
Add notes (optional) — jot a brief summary. Tap the microphone button to use voice-to-text instead of typing.
5
Save Call — tap the green button at the bottom. A confirmation screen will offer to log another call or return to the dashboard.
ℹ️A date & time is required when you choose Callback or Follow-up as the next action.
✨
5. AI Log Assistant

The AI Log Assistant lets you describe a call in plain language and automatically fills in the log form for you. Access it via the star icon in the Log a Call top bar, or from Settings.

1
Tap the ✨ icon in the top bar of Log a Call.
2
Type or speak a natural description of the call — e.g. "Called Sarah, no answer, will try again Friday afternoon."
3
The AI parses your description and pre-fills the person, result, next action, date, and notes.
4
Review the filled-in details and tap Confirm & Save.
💡Be specific about the person's name and any follow-up date — the AI will pick them up automatically.
📖
6. Past Notes

Past Notes shows the full call history for any person. Use it to review previous conversations before making a call.

1
Go to Past Notes from the Home screen.
2
Type a name in the search box to filter the contact list.
3
Tap a person's name to see every logged call — result, notes, and next steps — in reverse chronological order.
ℹ️You can also reach a person's history from the Who to Call dashboard by tapping their name.
🔍
7. Search

Search lets you find notes and calls across your entire contact list at once — useful when you remember something from a conversation but cannot recall who it was with.

Start typing and results from both names and call notes will appear. Tap a result to view that person's full history.

💡Search checks both contact names and the notes field, so you can search for keywords from a conversation.
📊
8. Analytics

The Analytics page gives you a bird's-eye view of your follow-up activity over time. It shows:

📈
Calls per week — a trend of how many calls you have logged each week.
✓
Reach rate — the percentage of attempts where you actually spoke to someone.
📅
Activity overview — a breakdown of call results (Reached, No Answer, Left Message, Rescheduled).

Tap Refresh in the top bar to load the latest data.

➕
9. Adding People

Add a new contact to your call list from Home → Add Person.

1
Full Name — required. This is how the person will appear throughout the app.
2
Role — optional. E.g. Cell Leader, Coordinator, Manager.
3
Fellowship — optional. The group, campus, or community they belong to.
4
Priority — optional. E.g. High, Medium, Low — helps you identify important contacts at a glance.
5
Call Cadence (days) — how many days between calls. Defaults to 28 days. Flock uses this to calculate when each person is next due.
6
Tap Add to Call List to save. The person will appear on the dashboard immediately.
💡You can edit a person's cadence and active status any time from Settings → People Settings.
⚙️
10. Settings

Access Settings from the ⚙ icon on the Home screen. There are two sub-sections:

①
Your Name — enter your name here so Flock can use it in greetings and email reminders.
App Settings
⏱
Reminders — configure whether you receive email reminders and at what time of day.
🌐
Timezone — set your local timezone so due dates and reminders are calculated correctly.
🕐
Hours — define your working hours so Flock does not schedule calls outside of them.
People Settings
📅
Cadence — adjust the number of days between calls for any individual. Type a new number and tap Save.
🔘
Active / Inactive — toggle someone inactive to remove them from the dashboard without deleting their history. Toggle them back on when ready to resume.
✎
Edit details — tap the pencil icon next to any person to update their name, role, fellowship, or priority.
ℹ️Changes to cadence take effect immediately — the dashboard will reflect new due dates the next time it loads.

You are all set — happy shepherding! 🕊
