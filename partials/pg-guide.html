Section 1

Overview

Flock is a pastoral follow-up tool designed to help you stay consistent with the people in your care.

It keeps track of who you reached, when someone is due next, and what was discussed, so your follow-up stays clear and nothing quietly drops off.

Each interaction you log updates the schedule automatically.

Flock works best when you log as you go, not later.
⚡
Section 2

Daily Flow

1
Open Who to Call and begin from the list, not from memory.
2
Start with Callbacks, then move to Overdue, then Due Today.
3
Log each interaction immediately so your dashboard stays accurate.
4
Set the next step before you leave the page.

Section 3

Home Screen

The Home screen is your starting point for the day. It highlights the main numbers that need your attention quickly.

Callbacks Overdue Due Today

Tap any stat card to jump straight into that section in Who to Call. From Home, you can also reach the rest of the app.

The Settings icon opens app preferences and people settings. The moon icon switches appearance mode.

📋
Section 4

Who to Call

This is your daily priority list. People are grouped by urgency so you know what to do first.

Callbacks Overdue Due Today This Week Not Yet Scheduled

Tap a person to take action - call, log, or schedule the next step. The list updates from your latest interaction and due date.

People scheduled further out stay hidden until they are closer, which keeps the page focused.

Work through Callbacks first, then Overdue, then Due Today.
📞
Section 5

Logging an Interaction

Log what happened, then set what comes next.

1
Select a person by searching their name. Their recent interactions appear for quick context.
2
Choose a result: Reached, No Answer, Left Message, or Rescheduled.
3
Set the next action: None, Callback, or Follow-up. If you choose Callback or Follow-up, add the date and time.
4
Add notes if needed. Keep them short and useful. You can also use voice-to-text.
5
Save and let Flock update the schedule automatically.
A date and time are required when the next action is Callback or Follow-up.
✨
Section 6

Smart Auto-fill

Describe the interaction naturally and Flock helps structure it for you.

1
Open the assistant from the star icon in the log page.
2
Type or speak what happened - for example: Called Sarah, no answer, will try again Friday afternoon.
3
Review the pre-filled person, result, notes, and next step.
4
Confirm and save.
Be clear about the person's name and the follow-up timing. That gives the best result.
📖
Section 7

Past Notes

Past Notes shows the full history for a person - calls, notes, outcomes, and follow-ups in one place.

Use it before making a call so your next conversation starts with context.

You can also reach someone's history directly from Who to Call.
🔍
Section 8

Search

Search helps you find people, notes, and past interactions quickly across the app.

You can search by name or by words you remember from a previous conversation.

📊
Section 9

Analytics

Analytics gives you a quick view of how consistent your follow-up has been over time.

📈
Calls per week shows your recent activity trend.
✓
Reach rate shows how often your attempts led to an actual conversation.
🗒
Activity overview breaks down results such as Reached, No Answer, Left Message, and Rescheduled.
➕
Section 10

Adding People

Add someone once, then update their details later whenever needed.

1
Full Name is required.
2
Role, Fellowship, and Priority are optional but useful.
3
Call Cadence controls how many days Flock leaves between follow-ups.
4
Save and the person appears in the system immediately.
⚙
Section 11

Settings

Settings lets you control how Flock works for you and how people are managed in the system.

👤
Your Name is used for greetings and reminders.
⏰
Reminders, Timezone, and Hours help Flock schedule correctly around your day.
🗒
Cadence can be adjusted per person whenever needed.
🔘
Active / Inactive lets you remove someone from the dashboard without deleting their history.
✎
Edit details to update a person's name, role, fellowship, or priority.
Changes to cadence take effect immediately.
Stay consistent - that's where the impact is. 🕊
